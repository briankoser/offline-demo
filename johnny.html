The Main in Beige

Johnny Cache was born in a small room, in a small town, in a small body. As he grew up, he found a love for western-country music. When he was 3, he saw his first horse. And there was no looking back.

Johnny was a western singer, and a country singer, which made him a western-country singer.

Johnny often wore all beige: beige shirt, beige jacket, beige pants, beige socks, a beige sweater if it was cold, beige shoes, beige hats on rainy days. Even beige underwear, it is rumored. He wore all beige to show solidarity with the working class man, who was boring.
