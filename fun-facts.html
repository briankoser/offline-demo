  2. The letter H is silent in the name John, unless you pronounce it Jone, which sounds like a girl’s name.
  3. Johnny Cache was born in a tree and raised by bees.
  4. My favorite Johnny Cache song is Beer For My Horses. I have it recorded off the radio on a cassette tape but I strangely can’t find it on the official albums, and I have all the official albums.
  5. Johnny Cache’s first wife was named May. His second wife was named June. His third wife was an alcoholic.
  6. When Johnny Cache gets serious, you should call him John Cache.
  7. The New Testament name John appears to be unrelated to the Old Testament name Jonah.
  8. There have been many famous Jonahs in history:
    1. The whale guy
    2. Jonah Bobo, from Zathura: A Space Adventure
    3. Jonah Hex
  9. There have been 22 popes named John, but there was no John XX. If they reach 30, they will also skip John XXX.
  10. I searched Twitter for “fun fact” and literally the second tweet was the one about no John XX.
  11. There was once a rapster named 50 Cent (no relation).
